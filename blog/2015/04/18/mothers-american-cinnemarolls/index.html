Mother's american cinnemarolls

Apr 18, 2015 · 1 minute read

Gives 30 rolls. Dough: * 50 g yeast * 1⁄2 cup warm water 115g * 1 1⁄2 cup milk, heated to boiling then cooled to luke warm 350g * 1⁄2 cup sugar 105g * 2 ts salt 3g * 1⁄2 cup butter 107g * 6 to 7 cups flour 800g

Raise for 1 hour.

Filling: * 8ss butter * 1 cup sugar * 8 ts cinnamon

Backed in the oven in a pan with baking paper. 220deg 20 mins
