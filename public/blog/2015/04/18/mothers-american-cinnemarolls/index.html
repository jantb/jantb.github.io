Mother's American cinnemarolls

Apr 18, 2015 · 1 minute read

Gives 30 rolls. Dough:

  • 50 g yeast
  • 1⁄2 cup warm water 115g
  • 1 1⁄2 cup milk, heated to boiling then cooled to luke warm 350g
  • 1⁄2 cup sugar 105g
  • 2 tsp salt 11g
  • 1⁄2 cup butter 107g
  • 7 to 8 cups flour 800g

Raise for 1 hour.

Filling:

  • 10 tbsp butter 50g
  • 1 cup sugar 210g
  • 8 tsp cinnamon 20g

Make 2 flat mats. Add filling, roll and cut. Raise another hour. Baked in the oven in a pan with baking paper. 220 °C 20 mins
